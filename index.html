Dark red — high rent, low income. The most rent-burdened states. Florida is the sole occupant of this cell.

Dark green — low rent, high income. No US state currently occupies this cell, a finding in itself.

Pale yellow — high rent, high income. Expensive but households can largely afford it (e.g. Hawaii, DC, New York).

Neutral grey — mid rent, mid income. The average American state.
